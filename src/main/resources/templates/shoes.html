Air Jordan I (Travis Scott edition)
Dressed in Earthy Brown tones, this Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 comes with Red Nike and Cactus Jack tongue labels. But the most notable feature is a backwards midfoot Nike Swoosh embroidered onto its lateral side, along with a hidden money velcro pocket on the inner liner. Other details includes the “Cactus Jack” logo behind the Swoosh on the medial side, while having a debossed face logo on the heels.
Air Jordan XI
The Air Jordan 11 debuted in 1995 as Michael Jordan's 11th signature sneaker. Designed by Tinker Hatfield, the Jordan 11 was the first Air Jordan to feature patent leather and carbon fiber in addition to cordura nylon. The original Jordan 11 was released from 1995-1996 in both mid cut and low cut IE colorways. The sneaker returned in 2000 for its first retro run and the debut of the patent leather low top version. The Air Jordan 11 continues to be one of the most popular Air Jordans of all-time with many retro releases in its vast catalog of colorways.
